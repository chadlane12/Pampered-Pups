About Us

Where your dog waggles more and barks less!

eversince I was a kid, dogs have been some of my best friends. I grew up helping my Aunt Maria in her dog grooming salon, The clip-n-Fluff. it was only natural that this love for pets would develop into my life's work. After college, I managed a local veterinary clnic for six years, until 2003, when my aunt was gettig ready to retire and wanted me to take over her business. I saw the need for a place where dogs of every breed and size could play and get some TLC while their families were at work or out of town. And so, that year, Pampered Pups was born. We've been pampering dogs ever since, and they haven't stopped wagging their tails!

Pampered Pups is just as popular with ownes as it with their pets. our service and hours are tailored to meet the needs of busy pet owners who want their"best friends" treated with the affection and care they get at home. Come see for yourself. Stop in for a vist, and bring your dog along with you.
